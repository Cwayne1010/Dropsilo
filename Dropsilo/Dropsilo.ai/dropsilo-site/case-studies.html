Financial Services

Automated Commercial Appraisal Ordering

A regional bank's commercial lending team was spending 4-6 hours per appraisal order — manually contacting appraisers, collecting quotes, comparing responses, and generating engagement letters. The process touched five people and three different systems.

What we built: An end-to-end automated workflow triggered by the loan origination system. Incoming appraisal requests are automatically routed, RFQ emails sent to qualified appraisers, quotes aggregated and ranked, and engagement letters generated — all without manual intervention.

Centralized Client Data Across 6 Platforms

A growing consulting firm tracked client information across their CRM, email, spreadsheets, invoicing software, project management tool, and shared drives. Updating one client record meant touching four different systems. New hires took weeks to figure out where anything lived.

What we built: A centralized data layer that syncs client information across all platforms in real time. Updates in any system propagate automatically. A single dashboard provides a unified client view with project status, billing, and communication history.

AI-Powered Document Processing Pipeline

A financial services company processed hundreds of compliance documents monthly — each requiring manual review, data extraction, and entry into their tracking system. The process was slow, error-prone, and consumed their most experienced team members' time.

What we built: An LLM-powered document pipeline that automatically classifies incoming documents, extracts key data fields, cross-references against existing records, flags anomalies for human review, and updates the tracking system — reducing a 45-minute task to under 3 minutes.
